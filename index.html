Welcome!

Game rules:

  • Your goal is to catch all the frogs on the screen
  • You can catch a frog by clicking on it - it will disappear!
  • When you will catch all the frogs on the screen - you will level up and your time will be added seconds equivalent to the the level number
  • The clock is ticking! Dont run out of time!
  • Ready? Press the start button to begin!
